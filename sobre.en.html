About

Patricia de Paiva e Castro is a translator, interpreter, and language teacher with over 20 years of experience. She holds a degree in Psychology from the University of São Paulo (USP) and in Computer Science from the United States, a postgraduate degree in Business Administration from CEAG-FGV, and additional studies in Psychology from UCLA. She is an ATA Accredited Translator (American Translators Association), credentialed for translation from English to Brazilian Portuguese. She has worked for over 12 years as a freelance translator in areas such as software localization, telecommunications, computing, business, finance, and banking, and has also served as a translation and interpretation coordinator. She lives and works in São Paulo, SP — Brazil.
