Policies

The policies below apply to all services provided by Patricia Castro (Patricia de Paiva e Castro, CNPJ 35.555.848/0001-61).

1. Refund and Dispute Policy

Translation: After delivery, the client has 7 (seven) days to request free revisions or corrections. Full refund only if the service has not been started. If the service has been partially executed, the refund is proportional to the work not performed.

Interpretation: Full refund if cancelled with more than 48 hours' notice. Cancellation within 48 hours is subject to a 50% charge of the contracted amount.

Lessons: The first lesson is fully refundable if cancelled with 24 hours' notice. After the lesson package has begun, the refund is proportional to the lessons not yet delivered.

Disputes: In case of disagreement, the client should first contact us by email or WhatsApp. If no resolution is reached within 15 days, the courts of São Paulo/SP may be invoked.

2. Cancellation Policy

Lessons: Cancellation or rescheduling must be done at least 24 hours in advance. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 50% charge of the lesson fee. No-shows incur the full charge.

Translation: Free cancellation before work begins. After commencement, a proportional charge applies based on progress already made.

Interpretation: Free cancellation with 48 hours' notice. Less than 48 hours: 50% of the fee. On the day of the event: 100%.

3. Legal Restrictions and Jurisdiction

Services are provided by Patricia de Paiva e Castro, registered under CNPJ 35.555.848/0001-61, based in São Paulo/SP, Brazil.

Jurisdiction: The courts of the district of São Paulo/SP are elected to resolve any disputes.

LGPD: The processing of personal data complies with Brazilian Law No. 13.709/2018 (LGPD). See our Privacy Policy.

Export: No export restrictions apply to the services provided, which consist of intellectual work in translation, interpretation, and teaching.

4. Promotions Terms

There are currently no active promotions. If promotions are offered, the specific terms and conditions of each promotion will be published on this page.

5. Statement descriptor
