Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data collected

When using the contact form on this website, the following personal data is collected:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Subject and content of the message

We do not collect browsing data, do not use tracking cookies, and do not use third-party analytics tools.

2. Purpose of processing

The data provided is used exclusively for:

  • Responding to your contact and providing information about the services
  • Delivering the contracted services (translation, interpretation, teaching)
  • Communications related to ongoing projects

3. Legal basis

The processing of personal data is based on the data subject's consent (Article 7, item I, of Brazilian Law No. 13.709/2018 — LGPD). Consent is given at the time the form is submitted, by explicitly checking the consent box.

4. Data sharing

Your personal data is not shared with third parties, except when strictly necessary for the provision of the contracted service (e.g., sending documents) and with your consent.

5. Data subject rights

Under Article 18 of the LGPD, you have the right to:

  • Confirm the existence of processing of your data
  • Access your data
  • Correct incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated data
  • Request anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary data
  • Request data portability to another provider
  • Revoke consent at any time

To exercise your rights, send an email to patricia@castrotranslations.net.
